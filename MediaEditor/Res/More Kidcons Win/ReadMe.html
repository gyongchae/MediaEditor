Introduction: The Kidcon collections have become quite popular among the online Mac community. The Iconfactory continues to get fan mail for these sets all the time. Its no surprise then that this new collection – More Kidcons, is the fourth in the series and picks up right where the last sets left off.

This all new “More Kidcons” icon collection contains 50 additional icons based on the Iconfactory’s popular Kidcon theme. The icons are set in bright, vibrant colors that kids love and are drawn with simple shapes to make spotting them on your Mac’s desktop easy. The set includes icons for back to school, sports, animals, around the home and some really fun icons from the “childhood games” realm. These icons are great for both children and adults. They make customizing your Macintosh fun, and using your computer a treat. We hope you enjoy our latest collection of More Kidcons!

Thanks: Thanks go out once again to my dear Mindy for her awesome brainstorm of the original Kidcons. They have become one of the most beloved collections at the Iconfactory. I love creating them because they are a prime example of my whole design philosophy… simple is better. Too much detail clutters the world of the visual sometimes, and the Kidcons are my occasional escape into the bliss of minimalism.
