From the Artist

August 1, 1997

Kidcon OS by Gedeon Maheux

Fast on the heels of the popular Kidcon icon collection comes the Kidcon OS set. These icons fill in the missing pieces for any user, child or adult, that wants to customize their mac in the Kidcon appearance. The set provides a number of folders for different aspects of your computer, all designed with the bold, striking lines and colors of Kidcons.

The enclosed folder contains over 55 custom Kidcon icons to customize your Mac. This fun and playful set was drawn by hand utilizing bold lines and striking colors to bring out the kid in everyone. There are Kidcon replacements for many of the standard folder types like “Applications” “Utilities” or “Games” in addition to missing system level icons like the “Generic Application” icon. Whether your eight or eighty, we’re sure you’ll enjoy the Kidcon OS Icon Collection as long as you’re young at heart!
